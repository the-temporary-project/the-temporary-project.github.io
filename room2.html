Madina Masimova

Urban Emotions




The first time I came to London was in the of summer 2019 to pursue my design and art passion. The city and people I met had a great influence on my creative vision and ideas. London showed me: the world needs my creativity. I came back in February 2020 as a study abroad student in Interior and Spatial Design at the University of the Arts London. This collection is a depiction of energy, inspiration and alongside architectural and spatial exploration of the United Kingdom.

London’s Rhyme reflects spirit of the city. Static architecture meets constant movement in the Thames waves, cloud floating and people’s life. For me, the most fascinating part was translation of spatial through architecture and art at the same time.

Festive London depicts The Royal Albert Hall in a colourful manner and highlights the beauty art adds to architecture. Feelings and emotions brought by Cirque du Soleil to the building can be found in warm coloured pencil touches.

These Architectural drawings are investigations of London’s character in the conjunction and combination of past and present. Moreover, they are memoires I have collected during my travelling in the United Kingdom.
