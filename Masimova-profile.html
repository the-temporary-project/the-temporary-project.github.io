Madina Masimova

Madina Masimova is an emerging Interior & Spatial Designer, Artist and Creator. Her studies at the University of the Arts London in Interior and Spatial Design had great influence on her design and art practice. She designs spaces and environments diving deep into the meaning of spatial and questioning it. Madina explores interior and spatial design through lenses of fine art, creative technology, photography and writing. Her practice focuses on creating spaces at the conjunction of science and art, taking into account its impact to the world. Madina is engaged into design in other planets and cosmos. In her art practice she portrays spatial and emotional transformations highlighting feelings in an expressive manner.
