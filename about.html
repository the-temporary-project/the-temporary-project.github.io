Our Ethos

Hi, artists and art-lovers! We are a new digital art gallery focussed on discovering non-mainstream, upcoming artists who have unique and powerful stories to tell.

It can be incredibly daunting for creatives thinking about starting a career as an artist - especially in todayâ€™s climate. We hope that by starting our own gallery we can offer a helping hand to those deciding to take that leap, whether you consider yourself to be an amateur, professional or hobbyist.

By building this platform we hope to break the self-perpetuating cycle of current art curation and create a supportive and represented community. The Temporary Project is a passion project run by a team of two (an art enthusiast and an aspiring artist) and we want to hear from YOU!
